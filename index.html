Frequently Asked Questions

codebar courses sessions are all virtual and can be attended from anywehere in the world.

Our courses are part time, allowing students to work a full time job alongside them.

Our AI Fundamentals course starts on May the 6th 2026 and is 8 weeks long.
Students have the expectation of attending a 1 or 2 hour virtual lunchtime session (12-1pm GMT) per week usually on Wednesdays and Fridays as well as completing an hour of homework per week.
The final session will be in a Wednesday evening (6pm-9pm GMT)

To attend our AI Fundamentals course you must have completed either codebar's 30 Days of Python course or DataCamp's Introduction to Python course.

As an existing codebar member you can request access to the data camp course by filling in this form
